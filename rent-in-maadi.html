Tucked along the east bank of the Nile River, Maadi is more than just a prestigious Cairo suburb—it's a sanctuary for those seeking tranquility, safety, and modern urban living. Maadi Neighborhood Guide: Degla vs. Sarayat

Degla and Sarayat are Maadi’s crown jewels, each with its own personality. Maadi Degla is a vibrant area where old meets new—classic villas sit side by side with modern apartment blocks. It’s ideal for women who want quick access to cafes, shops, and wellness centers without sacrificing peace. Degla is also well-lit at night, a critical consideration for women who walk or commute solo.

Maadi Sarayat, on the other hand, is older and more secluded, with charming brick-paved lanes and abundant green spaces. It appeals to professionals who prefer a quieter lifestyle and enjoy the occasional art exhibit or garden brunch. Rental homes in Sarayat are more traditional in design but offer unmatched tranquility.

Whether you prefer the social energy of Degla or the elegance of Sarayat, House Solution provides listings that cater specifically to these lifestyle preferences.
