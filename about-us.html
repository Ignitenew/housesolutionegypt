About Us

House Solution Egypt is a distinguished real estate agency based in Cairo, specializing in premier rental and sales services across the city’s most sought-after neighborhoods. With a primary focus on Maadi (including Sarayat, Degla, and Old Maadi), Katameya Heights, Zamalek, New Cairo, and other upscale districts, they offer an extensive portfolio of luxury properties such as apartments, villas, penthouses, duplexes, studios, ground floors, and office spaces.

Driven by a commitment to quality and excellence, House Solution Egypt meticulously vets each listing to ensure that clients experience the finest in modern living. Their offerings include fully furnished and semi-furnished units, ultra-modern designs, shared and private amenities like swimming pools, rooftop terraces, private gardens, and scenic views of the Nile and Pyramids. Whether matching a young family with a quiet, sunlit apartment in Maadi or assisting a discerning executive seeking a high-end penthouse in Zamalek, they tailor solutions to individual lifestyles and aspirations.

Beyond transactional efficiency, House Solution Egypt serves as a trusted advisor, guiding clients through market insights, neighbourhood benefits, and emerging rental and purchasing trends. Their informative blog, featuring guides such as “How to Find Rental Apartment in Maadi” and “Types of Rental Properties in Maadi,” illustrates their dedication to empowering clients with knowledge, simplifying decisions, and highlighting local nuances.

The real value delivered by House Solution Egypt resides in their seamless, comprehensive service: they bridge client needs with trusted landlords and sellers, streamline the search and acquisition processes, and offer unwavering support through every stage. By maintaining high standards for finish, furnishing quality, and location desirability, they ensure that clients not only find a property but secure a home or investment with confidence and satisfaction.
